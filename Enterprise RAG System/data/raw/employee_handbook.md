## Chapter 1: Welcome to TechCorp

### 1.1 Company Overview

TechCorp is a leading enterprise software company founded in 2015, headquartered in San Francisco, California. We specialize in AI-powered business solutions, serving over 500 enterprise clients globally.

Our mission: "Making complex technology simple for every business."

### 1.2 Our Values

**Innovation First**: We encourage experimentation. Failure is learning.

**Customer Obsession**: Every decision starts with "How does this help our customers?"

**Transparency**: We share information openly. No politics, no hidden agendas.

**One Team**: Engineering, Sales, Support — we succeed together or fail together.

### 1.3 Company Structure

TechCorp has 850 employees across 4 offices:
- San Francisco (HQ): 400 employees — Engineering, Product, Executive
- New York: 200 employees — Sales, Marketing, Customer Success
- London: 150 employees — EMEA Operations, Engineering
- Singapore: 100 employees — APAC Operations, Support

## Chapter 2: Employment Policies

### 2.1 Working Hours

**Standard Hours**: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM local time.

**Flexible Work Policy**: Core hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Outside core hours, employees may adjust their schedule with manager approval.

**Remote Work**: Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote positions require VP approval.

### 2.2 Time Off & Leave

#### Paid Time Off (PTO)
| Tenure | Annual PTO |
|--------|------------|
| 0-2 years | 15 days |
| 2-5 years | 20 days |
| 5+ years | 25 days |

PTO does not roll over. Maximum carryover: 5 days with manager approval.

#### Sick Leave
Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave annually. Sick leave does not roll over.

For absences exceeding 3 consecutive days, a doctor's note is required.

#### Parental Leave
- **Primary Caregiver**: 16 weeks fully paid
- **Secondary Caregiver**: 6 weeks fully paid
- Must be taken within 12 months of birth/adoption

#### Bereavement Leave
- Immediate family (spouse, parent, child, sibling): 5 days paid
- Extended family: 3 days paid

#### Jury Duty
Full pay for up to 10 days of jury duty per year.

### 2.3 Holidays

TechCorp observes the following paid holidays (US offices):
1. New Year's Day
2. Martin Luther King Jr. Day
3. Presidents' Day
4. Memorial Day
5. Independence Day
6. Labor Day
7. Thanksgiving Day
8. Day after Thanksgiving
9. Christmas Eve
10. Christmas Day

International offices follow local holiday calendars.

## Chapter 3: Compensation & Benefits

### 3.1 Pay Structure

**Pay Frequency**: Semi-monthly (15th and last day of month)

**Direct Deposit**: Required for all employees

**Pay Grades**:
| Grade | Title Range | Salary Range (USD) |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| L1 | Junior/Associate | $60,000 - $85,000 |
| L2 | Mid-level | $85,000 - $120,000 |
| L3 | Senior | $120,000 - $160,000 |
| L4 | Staff/Principal | $160,000 - $220,000 |
| L5 | Director | $200,000 - $280,000 |
| L6 | VP | $280,000 - $400,000 |

### 3.2 Equity

All full-time employees receive stock options:
- **Vesting Schedule**: 4 years with 1-year cliff
- **Grant Refresh**: Annual refresh grants based on performance
- **Exercise Window**: 10 years from grant date

### 3.3 Health Insurance

TechCorp offers comprehensive health coverage:

**Medical Plans**:
- PPO Premium: $50/month employee, $200/month family
- PPO Standard: $0/month employee, $100/month family
- HDHP + HSA: $0/month, company contributes $1,500/year to HSA

**Dental**: Delta Dental, $0/month
**Vision**: VSP, $0/month

**Coverage begins**: First day of employment

### 3.4 401(k) Retirement

- **Company Match**: 100% match up to 4% of salary
- **Vesting**: Immediate vesting on all contributions
- **Provider**: Fidelity Investments

### 3.5 Additional Benefits

**Learning & Development**: $2,000/year for courses, conferences, books

**Wellness Stipend**: $100/month for gym, fitness apps, mental health

**Home Office Stipend**: One-time $500 for home office setup

**Commuter Benefits**: Pre-tax transit and parking up to IRS limits

**Employee Referral Bonus**: $5,000 for engineering roles, $3,000 for others

## Chapter 4: Performance & Growth

### 4.1 Performance Reviews

**Cycle**: Bi-annual (February and August)

**Components**:
1. Self-assessment
2. Peer feedback (360-degree)
3. Manager assessment
4. Calibration across teams

**Ratings**:
- Exceptional (top 10%)
- Exceeds Expectations (top 30%)
- Meets Expectations (middle 50%)
- Needs Improvement (bottom 10%)

### 4.2 Promotion Process

Promotions are considered during February and August review cycles.

**Requirements**:
- Minimum 12 months at current level
- Consistently performing at next level for 6+ months
- Manager nomination and skip-level approval
- L4+ requires VP approval, L5+ requires C-suite approval

### 4.3 Career Tracks

TechCorp offers two career tracks:

**Individual Contributor (IC) Track**:
Junior → Mid → Senior → Staff → Principal → Distinguished

**Management Track**:
Team Lead → Manager → Senior Manager → Director → VP → C-Suite

Employees may switch tracks with manager approval.

## Chapter 5: Code of Conduct

### 5.1 Workplace Behavior

TechCorp is committed to a respectful, inclusive workplace.

**Zero Tolerance**: Harassment, discrimination, bullying, or retaliation of any kind.

**Protected Classes**: Race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, national origin, age, disability, veteran status.

**Reporting**: Report concerns to your manager, HR, or ethics@techcorp.com. Anonymous reporting available through EthicsPoint hotline.

### 5.2 Conflicts of Interest

Employees must disclose:
- Outside employment or consulting
- Financial interest in competitors, vendors, or customers
- Family relationships with other TechCorp employees

Submit disclosures to HR within 30 days of the conflict arising.

### 5.3 Confidentiality

All employees sign NDAs upon hire.

**Confidential Information Includes**:
- Product roadmaps and unreleased features
- Customer data and contracts
- Financial information
- Employee personal information
- Trade secrets and proprietary algorithms

**Retention**: Confidentiality obligations continue for 2 years after employment ends.

### 5.4 Social Media Policy

Employees may discuss TechCorp publicly but must:
- Clearly state opinions are their own
- Not disclose confidential information
- Not speak on behalf of TechCorp without PR approval

## Chapter 6: IT & Security

### 6.1 Equipment

**Provided Equipment**:
- Laptop (MacBook Pro or Dell XPS, employee choice)
- Monitor (up to 2 external monitors)
- Keyboard, mouse, headset

**Equipment Refresh**: Every 3 years or as needed for role requirements

**Return Policy**: All equipment must be returned within 7 days of last day

### 6.2 Acceptable Use

Company equipment and networks are for business use.

**Prohibited**:
- Illegal activities
- Downloading unauthorized software
- Accessing inappropriate content
- Cryptocurrency mining
- Circumventing security controls

**Monitoring**: TechCorp reserves the right to monitor company equipment and networks.

### 6.3 Security Requirements

**Password Policy**:
- Minimum 12 characters
- Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols
- Change every 90 days
- No password reuse (last 10 passwords)

**Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)**: Required for all systems

**VPN**: Required when accessing company resources remotely

**Security Training**: Annual security awareness training mandatory

### 6.4 Incident Reporting

Report security incidents immediately to security@techcorp.com or Slack #security-incidents.

**Incidents Include**:
- Lost or stolen devices
- Suspected phishing
- Unauthorized access
- Data breaches
- Malware

## Chapter 7: Travel & Expenses

### 7.1 Travel Policy

**Booking**: Use Concur for all travel booking

**Approval Requirements**:
- Domestic travel: Manager approval
- International travel: VP approval
- Travel > $5,000: Finance approval

**Advance Notice**: Book at least 14 days in advance when possible

### 7.2 Expense Limits

| Category | Limit |
|----------|-------|
| Airfare (domestic) | Economy class |
| Airfare (international 6+ hours) | Premium economy |
| Hotel | Up to $250/night |
| Meals | $75/day |
| Ground transport | Reasonable (Uber/Lyft preferred) |

### 7.3 Reimbursement

Submit expenses within 30 days via Concur.

**Required Documentation**:
- Itemized receipts for expenses > $25
- Business purpose
- Attendee list for meals with others

**Reimbursement Timeline**: 5-7 business days after approval

## Chapter 8: Separation

### 8.1 Resignation

**Notice Period**: 2 weeks minimum (4 weeks for L4+)

Submit resignation in writing to your manager and HR.

### 8.2 Termination

TechCorp is an at-will employer.

**Severance** (involuntary termination without cause):
- 2 weeks base pay per year of service
- Minimum 4 weeks, maximum 16 weeks
- COBRA coverage for severance period

### 8.3 Exit Process

1. Return all company equipment
2. Complete exit interview with HR
3. Disable access to all systems
4. Final paycheck includes unused PTO
5. Provide forwarding address for tax documents
