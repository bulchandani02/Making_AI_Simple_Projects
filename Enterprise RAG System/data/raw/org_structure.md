## Reporting Structure Principles

1. **Span of Control**: Managers typically have 5-10 direct reports
2. **Skip Levels**: Encouraged for feedback, career discussions
3. **Matrix Reporting**: Some roles (e.g., Solutions Engineers) have dotted lines
4. **Remote Employees**: Report to nearest office manager when possible

## Career Levels

### Individual Contributors

| Level | Title Pattern | Years Exp (typical) |
|-------|---------------|---------------------|
| L1 | Junior/Associate | 0-2 years |
| L2 | [Role] | 2-4 years |
| L3 | Senior [Role] | 4-7 years |
| L4 | Staff [Role] | 7-10 years |
| L5 | Principal [Role] | 10+ years |

| Level | Title Pattern | Scope |
|-------|---------------|-------|
